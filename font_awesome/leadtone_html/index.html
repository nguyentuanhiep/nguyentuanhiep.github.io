About

Who we are?

Leadtone Holdings Limited is an organization exporting agricultural products from New Zealand. We also work as a reference to provide all information and statistic about agricultural sector. Together with understanding about New Zealand agriculture industry, you would be provided with trustworthy information about its products and all evidences to prove their premium in the international market. With aim at extending the market, Leadtone now has a representative office in Hanoi, Vietnam.

What we do?

Leadtone not only brings you the high quality products from New Zealand which is named as one of the happiest countries in the world, because of the quality of environment, but we also give you a deeper understanding about how qualitative they are. So as to do this, we have been making effort to seek the chances to cooperate with Kiwi farmers to have a sustainable good flow. Besides, we keep searching out the knowledge, credible information and analysis you need to make the best decisions for your healthy life.
